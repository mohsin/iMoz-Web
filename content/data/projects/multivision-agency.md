Developed a professional marketing agency website for MultiVision Agency using the Nuxt framework.

## Features

The site is primarily a single-page application with various sections including a services overview, team introduction, and a separate client testimonials showcase page. A dark mode toggle allows visitors to browse in their preferred theme. The design is fully responsive across all device sizes.
