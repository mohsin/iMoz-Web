An Android app built for internal use by TheHouseMonk (now known as Cozee Homes), a Bangalore-based real estate startup.

## How It Started

The day I moved to Bangalore, I had already set up a meeting with the founder — who found me through a Quora answer. Fortunately, I had exactly the skills they were looking for: reactive programming for Android with RxAndroid.

## What Was Built

Built using Java with Dagger for dependency injection and RxAndroid for reactive data flows. The app helped property agents manage inventory, track deals, and communicate with clients.
