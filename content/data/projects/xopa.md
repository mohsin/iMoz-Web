X0PA AI is a Singapore-based data analytics startup that contacted me after their team couldn't solve a problem for over a week. A colleague from my alma mater recommended me, and when I got the task I solved it in under 2 hours.

## What Was Built

They then asked for a demo for parallel processing of R scripts for their attrition and retention predictor, and later asked to build ReactJS components to improve the platform.

Built interactive data analysis interfaces displaying employee risk scores, historical trends, and retention recommendations. Components integrate with R-based statistical models, enabling data scientists to visualize complex analyses in accessible dashboards.

The platform predicts employee attrition with up to 95% accuracy and job performance with 72% accuracy, helping companies reduce voluntary turnover by up to 25%.

I was offered a full-time remote employee role but declined due to disagreements with the contract agreement.
