An OctoberCMS plugin providing carousel and slider functionality for websites, built on top of Twitter Bootstrap's carousel component.

Offers extensive customization options for transition effects, timing, navigation controls, and indicators. Has accumulated over 6,000 documented installs on the OctoberCMS marketplace.
