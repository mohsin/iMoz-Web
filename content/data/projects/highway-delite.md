"Explore highways like never before."

Built the entire platform — frontend website, authenticated backend, RESTful API, and Android app — single-handedly for a highway travel discovery startup.

## Web Platform

The backend includes a user management section, a custom permissions architecture, short URL management, and a map-based establishment manager where authorized users can add and edit highway stops directly on the map.

## Android App

Built a full-featured highway travel app that became the go-to companion for Indian road trips. It lets users discover restaurants, hotels, fuel stations, and other highway establishments along their route.

The app reached 50,000+ users in 6 months with a 4.7-star Play Store rating.

## Project History

In July 2016, due to a breach of contract, a copyright request was filed leading Google to take down the app. The app was later restored after the client settled and purchased the IP entirely.
