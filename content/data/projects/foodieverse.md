title: Foodieverse Android App
---

Built incremental features to the Foodieverse Android app — specifically a QR scanner that enables quick tagging of food items for their AirKitchens app.

## QR Scanner Feature

Implemented using Jetpack Compose, MLKit, and the Camera 2 API. Kitchen staff can scan QR codes on ingredients to instantly tag and track them within the AirKitchens inventory system, streamlining meal planning workflows.
