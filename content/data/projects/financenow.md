A finance management app that lets you input your financial data to get insights on your financial health.

## Features

- **Survival calculator** — how many months your savings will last
- **Insurance analysis** — how much coverage you need based on your profile
- **Financial wellness score** — overall health assessment with actionable recommendations

Built for Android using Java with RxAndroid reactive patterns.
