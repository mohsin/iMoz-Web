Built several backend components and features for ViewRetreats, a Melbourne-based wellness retreat booking platform specializing in curated spa and wellness holidays across Australia, New Zealand, and Asia.

## Modules Built

- **ZohoCRM integration** — synchronizes customer and booking data for relationship management
- **Checkout integration** — handles booking payment flows and confirmation
- **Expedia integration** — expanded inventory reach via Expedia connectivity
- **Gifting module** — enables users to purchase retreat experiences as gifts for others
