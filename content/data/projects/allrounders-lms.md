Built a comprehensive LMS platform enabling online learning with world-class instructors.

## Platform Features

- **Course management** — multi-course curriculum with certificate and degree tracking
- **Student dashboard** — progress tracking, course enrollment, and certificate generation
- **Instructor panel** — course creation, content management, and student performance analytics
- **Payments & notifications** — secure authentication, payment processing, and automated email notifications

Built with a Laravel backend handling all business logic and database operations, with a responsive Bootstrap frontend.
