Contributed features to Struct, a knowledge-rich AI-powered chat platform designed for open source communities.

## Features Built

- **Global command palette** — quick access to platform functions and AI assistants from anywhere in the app
- **Search** — advanced search across messages and files with filtering and sorting
- **Containerization** — web app optimizations for consistent, reproducible deployments

Struct's AI bot can ingest codebases, documentation, and blogs to provide contextually accurate responses, with customizable feeds enabling users to follow projects, teams, or tasks uniquely suited to their workflow.
